Our Story

Hi — we're a coffee shop in
Ubud building something
worth the wait.

We're in Ubud, doing what we love: making great coffee, coming up with drinks that actually surprise people, and finding the right beans to make it all work. We have a soft spot for high altitude naturals from Indonesia and South America — but mostly we just want it to taste good.

Our webstore is coming. While we're putting the finishing touches on it, you can still get our beans — we're shipping globally on request. Just message us on Instagram and we'll sort you out.

What we make

Our Specials

🍫

Dark & Intense

Espresso Nero

Espresso with 70% dark Italian chocolate melted in. Rich and complex — no sweetness added, none needed.

🍨

Sweet Treat

Affogato Nero

The Espresso Nero poured over fior di latte gelato. Same drink, colder, sweeter, a little more of a moment.

🍯

Iced & Creamy

Xtra Dirty Latte

Iced espresso with honey, finished with a sweet cinnamon cream on top.

🍋

Cold & Citrusy

Aerocano Limon

Iced espresso and lemon juice, with air worked in through the steam wand. Light, cold, and a little effervescent.

🍰

Dessert in a Cup

Tiramisu Macchiato

Ristretto and steamed milk, topped with tiramisu cream (ladyfingers, cacao, sugar). Comes with ladyfingers for dipping.

🍍

House Signature

Pertama

Espresso and fruit juice — we tried orange, cranberry, strawberry, apple, and pineapple. Pineapple was the most balanced, so that's the default. Ask to swap if you want.
